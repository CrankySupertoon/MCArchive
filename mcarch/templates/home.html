Minecraft Archive

Welcome to the the Minecraft Archive. Our goal is to make it easy to find old Minecraft mods. To that end, we archive mods, and keep them publicly accessible even after their creators no longer distribute them.

We provide downloads for mods which are no longer available for download from their original creator. Otherwise, we just link to the official download page.

If you want help with these mods or others for older versions of Minecraft, or you want to help us preserve these mods for the future, come join us on Discord.
